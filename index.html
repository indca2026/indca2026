Concept Note

The contemporary India-Central Asia relations are undergoing a profound and structural change due to dynamic international politics. Central Asia, the extended neighbourhood of India has assumed a status as a core ‘strategic periphery’ in terms of energy security, logistics connectivity, commerce, cultural and academic engagements.


The region is striving to balance the extra-territorial powers, namely Russia, China and the USA, utilising a multi-vector foreign policy. These geopolitical dimensions play a vital role in India-Central Asia relations in contemporary times.
